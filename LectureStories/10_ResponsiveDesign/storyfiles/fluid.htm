Fluid Layouts

Dimensions are determined by percentages not pixel measurements.

You might have a content column with a width that is 70% of the whole container and a navigation sidebar that occupies 25% with a 5% spacer column.

Everything will scale with the browser size.

Things may look ok on a 1024px laptop display and on a 768px tablet.

They might not work so well on a 2048px desktop display or on an iPhone.
