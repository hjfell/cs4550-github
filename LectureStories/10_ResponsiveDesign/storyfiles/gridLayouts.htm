Grid Layouts

  • help with balance, spacing, and organization
  • keep things readable and uncluttered
  • let your audience predict where things will be
  • make it easier to keep things consistent
  • make for easier collaboration without compromising consistency

Let the content (e.g. ads, images, text, videos, audio players) define the grid.

Ads or images might be the items that force the grid layout.
