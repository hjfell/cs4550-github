Breakpoints

Breakpoints are browser widths where we want to change the layout.

There are some standard breakpoints, e.g. 320px (iPhone), 768px (iPad), and 1024px (many laptop displays).

There are many others and they will all be subject to change.

What actually matters will depend on your material, e.g. the widths of images, buttons, forms, etc. in your pages.

Test for breakpoints by resizing the browser window. Watch how things change when resizing and add a breakpoint whenever the design breaks.

media_query_bookmarklet is a cool tool that shows the window dimensions as you resize your browser.
