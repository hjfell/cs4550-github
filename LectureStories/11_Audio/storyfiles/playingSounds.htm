Playing Sounds

Using href to play a sound jd_3921.mp3 


	<a href="../sounds/jd_3921.mp3">jd_3921.mp3</a>


Using embed to play a sound 


	<embed height="15" width="300"  src="../sounds/jd_3921.mp3" />

With autostart = "false" 


	<embed height="50" width="250"  style="background-color:pink;"  
		src="../sounds/jd_3921.mp3" autostart="false"  />

Using the HTML5 audio tag 


<audio controls>
  <source src="../sounds/jd_3921.ogg" type="audio/ogg">
  <source src="../sounds/jjd_3921.mp3" type="audio/mpeg">
Your browser does not support the audio element.
</audio>
