HTML Tests

Tire Sizing by Sheldon Brown appears on the site sheldonbrown.com. I have used the content of this article for some experiments with simple HTML. You can bring up this article in your browser and also view its source.

In this version on tire-sizing-01, I have used mostly simple HTML tags, e.g. <p>, <h1>, with no formatting attributes. Everything is flush left and the tables, except for the first, have no borders.

In this version of tire-sizing-02, I have added inline styles attributes to many of the tags. I also put in anchor tags and made the navigation elements at the top of the article work. Some of the changes you should see are:

  • Some of the headings and text are centered.
  • Some images are centered and others float to the right of the text.
  • The tables are centered and have borders.
  • The title font color is red.
