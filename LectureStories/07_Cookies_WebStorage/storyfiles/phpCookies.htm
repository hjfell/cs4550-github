PHP Cookies in a Login Page

You have already created a login page - or something like that - for your reservation system.

Now we add cookies to keep track of the fact that the user is already logged in.

These are the pieces we need:

  • A form for submitting information
  • A validation routine to check information
  • A database query to compare submitted information against stored information
  • Cookies or sessions to store data that reflects a successful login
  • A logging-out process, to clear out the cookies or session data (not shown below)

material from Larry Ullman, PHP and MySQL for Dynamic Web Sites, Fourth Edition
