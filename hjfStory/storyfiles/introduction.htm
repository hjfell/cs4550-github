Harriet Fell's First Story

This is the picture of me that appears on the CCIS faculty site.

I joined the Northeastern University Faculty in September 1971 as an assistant professor in the math department. In 1982, I left the math department along with Professors Casey, Chan, Proulx, Rasala, and Salzberg to help found the College of Computer Science.

Here I am working on the innards of my Mac Quadra.
